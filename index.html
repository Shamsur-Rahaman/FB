Welcome

FB Footwear Limited is 100% Export oriented footwear manufacturing Company. FB Footwear inaugurated its journey on 2006 using European-origin machinery and technical know-how. A widely spaced factory complying with up-to-date international specification, world class factory system with own power, warehousing facilities make it a highly sophisticated, environment friendly footwear unit. High standards of management and corporate strategy in addition to local expatriate qualified technicians make production and development which reflects on achievement of the annual turnover of $ 44.5 Million. It is ideally located within easy accessibility of transportation and only an hour away from the airport out of the city’s bustling crowd.
